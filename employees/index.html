The Illustrious Leader 516

The most cleverest of the JMC crew and its illustrious leader; the self proclaimed "kind of a big deal." 516 was the first to take the JMC throne post apocalypse. Umlaut 148

Vinegar (pronounced "Vine Gar") Strokes was meant to serve as a corporate translator and communications specialist. Repeated clonings have made his basic speech... less than understandable. Switching between multiple accents and mispronouncing most words have become the norm. Most clones call him "Umlaut" for his penchant to use seemingly unneeded accent marks on damn near everything.
